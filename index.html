About Your Instructor

My name is Tanveer and I am a fully qualified DVSA-approved driving instructor based in Edinburgh. I have extensive experience teaching learners of all abilities and stay up to date with the latest DVSA training methods.


I provide a relaxed, friendly, and patient learning environment. I believe learning to drive should be an enjoyable experience, not something stressful or overwhelming. Bringing my Bengali heritage to my work, I am proud to offer a heartfelt, human, and professional approach to every student.


I specialize in helping nervous students overcome anxiety, ensuring they feel safe, confident, and fully prepared for their life on the road.
