Meet Tanveer

I am a fully qualified DVSA-approved driving instructor based in Edinburgh. With years of experience teaching learners of all abilities, I stay current with the latest training methods to ensure the highest standard of instruction.


I provide a relaxed, friendly, and patient learning environment. I believe learning to drive should be an enjoyable experience, not something overwhelming. Bringing my Bengali heritage to my work, I offer a heartfelt, human approach. I am proud to serve the community and can offer instruction in Bengali for students who feel more comfortable in their native tongue.


Whether you are a complete beginner or looking for refresher lessons, I am dedicated to helping you become a safe, confident driver for life.
